SAT Mastery — Quick Fix

Seeing a white/blank screen on iPhone?

This can happen after an update if your iPhone saved an older version. Follow the steps below to refresh to the newest version.

1
Open the website in Safari
Tap the button below (it opens the latest version).
2
Remove the old Home Screen app (if installed)
Press and hold the icon → Remove App.
3
Re-install the newest version
In Safari: Share → Add to Home Screen.
Open SAT Mastery in Safari Open the landing page

Tip: If it still shows blank, go to Settings → Safari → Advanced → Website Data, search for the site, delete it, then try again.
